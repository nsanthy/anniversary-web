a letter to Anno 💌

A little surprise gift for you, to celebrate our first anniversary. Hopefully, the first of many more to come.

Knowing you and spending time with you has been one of the greatest joys of my life. You inspire me to grow into a better version of myself (I mean, I even dared to make this because of you 😊).

I am thankful for you.

You’re kind, smart, funny, and handsome. I wish you could see yourself the way I see you—then you’d understand how truly charming you are. And if others can’t see that, that’s their loss. I know I’m the lucky one, because I have you.

I am thankful for us.

Our relationship hasn’t been perfect — but I’ve come to realize that perfection doesn’t exist. And honestly, I love that. I love that we’re imperfect, that we sometimes argue, that we sometimes stumble. Because those moments, too, are part of our journey. They’ve helped us learn, helped us grow — not just as a couple, but as two people walking this path together.

What I want you to know is this: even through the rough patches, I love you. And if I were given the chance to relive my life and choose again, I would still choose you, every single time.

Always.

I love you.

xx
